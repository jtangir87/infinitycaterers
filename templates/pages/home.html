Services

Infinity Caterers is a full service event catering company serving Southeastern PA and New Jersey. We take pride in our commitment to excellence, quality, and most importantly, customer service!

Menu Planning

Infinity Caterers can personalize a detailed menu to fit any vision and budget. We’ll work directly with you to choose all the right food and orchestrate a service to make your event special!

First we’ll meet in person to discuss your event, review pictures and ideas for the food. We’ll then work out a menu that satisfies your expectations within a budget that you’ll be comfortable with. There’s hundreds of food options to choose from and we can create any favorites you have in mind.

Event Staffing

Infinity Caterers offers full service staffing for any type of event. Our trained staff are professionally dressed in all black attire and serve your guests with a smile and friendly manner.

Our head chefs, sous chefs, and even Sushi chefs are experts at crafting exquisite and delicious cuisine that will delight your guests and leave them happy and satiated. We have event supervisors who’ll make sure your occasion goes off without a hitch. Finally, our wait staff and bartenders will be sure to cater to all of your guest’s food and beverage desires.

Room Design & Layout

Infinity Caterers will diagram a floor plan and lay out for your event. We’ll then execute it to perfection!

We’ll visit your chosen venue to complete a full walk thru and strategize how to utilize the space to its fullest potential. Based on the visit, we’ll create a floor plan diagram to determine the appropriate layout for your event’s specific theme and action. We’ll then work with you to finalize all the necessary details and work out the logistics for bringing your vision to life.

Full Service Party Rentals

Infinity Caterers can assist will all rental equipment necessary to realize your event vision. Let us do all the legwork!

We’ll source and order all rentals including china, silverware, glassware, ovens, warming cabinet, tables, chairs, linens, and more. Let our decades of experience planning and sourcing anything an event may require. We can bring any idea you may have to life and make your occasion super special.
