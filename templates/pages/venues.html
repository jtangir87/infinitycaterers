Beat Street

Located right outside of Philadelphia in Huntingdon Valley, Beat Street features a unique Pop Art ambiance with fun features and layouts that can accommodate any type of event.

Beat Street has a funky warehouse look with neon lighting and exposed duct work. The venue offers two room options. The larger room holds 150-200 guests with a separate area for kids cocktails and lunch/dinner. The smaller “Industry” room offers has the same funky warehouse look for a capacity of 100-110 guests.
