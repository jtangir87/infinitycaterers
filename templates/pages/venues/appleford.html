Appleford Estate

Appleford is best known as the premier Main Line venue for beautiful wedding ceremonies and receptions. The estate has been used as a wedding venue creating lasting memories for more than 40 years.

While weddings are a major part of our business, Appleford in Villanova also hosts numerous events and social gatherings throughout the year. Appleford events include corporate dinners, receptions, holiday parties, wine tastings milestone celebrations, showers, and so much more.
