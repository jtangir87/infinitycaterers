Beat Street

Located right outside of Philadelphia in Huntingdon Valley, Beat Street features a unique Pop Art ambiance with fun features and layouts that can accommodate any type of event.

Beat Street was built focused on creating amazing events. Built in state of the art sound system and a nightclub style light show, the party is already here. A retro arcade game room for kids allows them to be kids, while the adults can grab a drink and Hhors d'oeuvres while mingling in the main reception area. Once the party gets underway and you work up an appetite, the kids and adults can have separate dining areas, so the entertainment can keep the kids going while adults take their time! With our open floor plan and Live Feed cameras, no matter where you are in the reception area you can always see what is happening on the dance floor.
