Loft At Passyunk

Set in the contemporary surroundings of the East Passyunk Ave area, Loft at Passyunk provides you with everything you need to make all your wedding dreams come true.

This hidden gem of a venue is a beautiful 2 story studio that boasts nearly 10,000 square feet of stunning event space including beautiful hardwood floors and large floor to ceiling windows overlooking the trendy East Passyunk Avenue and offering open light filled rooms with a guest capacity of 150.
