Congregation Or Ami

Located in Lafayette Hill, Pennsylvania, the Congregation Or Ami synagogue offers a large event space with a guest capacity of 250. This newly renovated space is the perfect blank canvas for you to create the perfect party.

In addition to the main event space, there’s a smaller side space for a kids cocktail and/or lunch/dinner. There’s also an additional large downstairs cocktail space for adults.
