Society Hill Dance Academy

Set in the quaint and romantic surroundings of the historic Headhouse Square, Society Hill Dance Academy provides you with everything you need to make all your wedding dreams come true.

This hidden gem of a venue is in the historic Headhouse Square of the Society Hill area of Philly. It is a certified historic Philadelphia building with loads of charm! Located at 2nd & Pine Streets, this venue offers open light filled rooms and has a guest capacity of 150.
